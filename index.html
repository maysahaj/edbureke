FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about studying in Germany and our services.

Is studying in Germany free?
Most public universities in Germany offer tuition-free education for international students. You only need to pay a small semester fee for administration and public transport.
Do I need German language skills?
It depends on your program. Many Master's programs are taught in English, but for Bachelor's degrees, you usually need a B2 or C1 level in German. We help you find the right language path.
How long does the application process take?
Typically, the process takes between 3 to 6 months from document preparation to receiving your admission and visa.
Do you help with visa applications?
Yes, our Advanced and Premium packages include full guidance for the German student visa, including blocked account setup and document review.
What if I don't get accepted?
Our evaluation process is very strict to ensure you only apply where you have a high chance. If a rejection occurs, we analyze the reasons and apply to alternative universities.
Can you help with accommodation in Germany?
Yes, we provide resources and guidance on how to find student dorms or private apartments in different German cities.
